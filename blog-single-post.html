Peachy makeup goodness

In this article I will tell you how to achieve a perfect peachy makeup result without using any expensive or exclusive products that I’m sure you will find in the nearest beauty shop in your city or town. If it is helpful to know what products I use and you are interested in purchasing any of them, please contact me directly. Doing this will increase my ability to share more frequent tips and tricks on my blog and Instagram. Thank you, genuinely, for reading my blog and for your support. Don’t forget to check out the full photoshoot in my website gallery.

Here’s what I used for eyes: MAC eyeshadows in Omega, Blanc Type and Brun. Brush 213, Brush 242, and Brush 239. Bobbi Brown eyeliner in Black Ink and Ultra Fine brush. Lancome Hypnose mascara. Urban Decay Waterline Eye Pencil in Legend and Bambi lashes . MAC Brun eyeshadow for brows using MAC angel brush 266.

Face, however, Required another selection of makeup tools: Temptu foundation, contour and bronzer (can be used with airbush compressor or just applied with a brush like this It Cosmetics brush. ) Blush is Tarte Fearless, and concealer is Tarte Creaseless concealer in Light-Medium Sand (both have a dewey finish.) Lips are Lancome Jeweled Pink lipstick and Lancome O So Coral La Laque Fever gloss (this gloss stays on well.) Urban Decay Bang lip liner.

Conclusion

As you see, this style of makeup is almost natural as it requires just some of the makeup tools and products that I hope you already have in your collection. The missing ones can be easily bought at the nearest makeup shop or by directly contacting me.

If you like such makeup and want me to share more of my personal and professional experiences, feel free to leave your suggestions in comments down below. I will be glad to share with you stories of my customers and how we achieved a perfectly looking makeup together.
