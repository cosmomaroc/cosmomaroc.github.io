History

2004, jessica priston

Jessica Priston started her career in 2004 with primary focus on providing high quality makeup services to corporate and individual customers. With more than 10 years of experience in makeup industry, Jessica quickly became known in the world of fashion and beauty.

Since childhood, she was always attracted by fashion and glamour as her mother was TV host and top model. Jessica first achieved success in 1992, after finishing fashion college in NYC,where she was born and raised. She received her first award in 16, for an exceptional makeup project.

What I love most about my job is my ability to empower and help women feel better about themselves. I am really happy that I achieved great success with my freelance business as it is my true passion. I also continue to learn more about makeup industry.

Jessica’s story

Jessica was born in the USA and was raised in the fashion industry through my mother, who was a model. She followed in her steps, and in 1990s during her time in modeling, she went to school in Florida to obtain a license as a makeup artist and hair stylist.

Make-up is Jessica’s passion, and she loves bringing out the best out of her clients. No matter the occasion, you will look and feel amazing. She has been working in the industry as a professional hair and makeup artist since 1999 and have gained immense knowledge .

Jessica has always been highly involved in the fashion industry, while doing makeup for models and artists.

2005-2015, PROSPEROUS YEARS

After opening her own freelance makeup practice for fashion industry, corporate events and movie studios, Jessica gained a lot of experience in various styles of makeup. According to her words, makeup is about art, creation and the power to bring about a transformation.

Since 2005, Jessica has worked with hundreds of clients, both corporate and individual, delivering makeup services of high quality and variety. She was awarded with “The Best Promising Makeup Artist” award in 2006, and she was also recognized by International Movie Association in 2012.
