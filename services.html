Services

Jessica Priston is glad to invite you to be pampered with the best makeup services in the USA. At her studio, you can have your makeup professionally applied, as you like it. Whether you’ve got a special event or small soiree, she will apply your makeup perfectly matched to any occasion.

Ms. Priston is dedicated to the art of makeup, offering both complimentary walk-in demos, and applications as well as lessons by appointment. She has undergone a rigorous certification process. Jessica will tailor each service with personalized looks and colours to celebrate your individuality.

Consultations

Taking a makeup consultation at Jessica’s studio is the best way to decide which makeup to apply. The guiding aim in all her makeup experiences is to understand your needs and desires and work out a way to make them come true.
