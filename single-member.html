Biography

+1 (800) 123 1334

info@demolink.org

Motivated, dedicated, kind and professional makeup artist, Jessica Priston began her beauty career at a young age when she was hired for her first makeup job at age seventeen just after graduating from New York Makeup College.

Her passion and aptitude for beauty have been clearly apparent her entire life and she chased her dreams of being a pro makeup artist to Los Angeles where she was trained at the prestigious Make-Up Designory. Upon completion of her professional training, Ms.Priston commenced her career working with professional photographers. Totally addicted to her job, you will find Jessica making the world a more beautiful place elbow deep in makeup, researching makeup, and experimenting with makeup.
